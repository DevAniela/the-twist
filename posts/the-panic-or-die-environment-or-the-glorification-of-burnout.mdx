## H2

In the beginning, I was more relaxed and confident. I put my heart and soul into everything I did. By the end, I was panicky and kind of had the subconscious expectation that if it's not hurried and last-minute, it's not good enough. If you're not exhausted by the end of the day, whatever you did that day was not worth it.
